Mattress Zones

Bonded Mattress

Bonded mattress is exclusively designed to provide firm support to your spine. It provides long-lasting relief from back aches and spine problems.

  • ✔️ Premium Quilted Fabric
  • ✔️ PU Foam
  • ✔️ HDPE Sheet
  • ✔️ Bonded Foam

Coir Mattress

Coir mattresses are eco-friendly and provide excellent ventilation. Perfect for those who prefer firm surfaces with natural materials.

  • ✔️ Natural Coir
  • ✔️ Breathable Material
  • ✔️ Anti-fungal Properties
  • ✔️ Durable Base

Foam Mattress

Foam mattresses offer great support and body contouring. Ideal for pressure relief and a soft feel throughout the night.

  • ✔️ High-Density Foam
  • ✔️ Lightweight & Durable
  • ✔️ No Motion Transfer
  • ✔️ Anti-Allergic

Spring Mattress

Spring mattresses are known for bounce and breathability. Engineered with pocket or bonnell springs to enhance comfort.

  • ✔️ Pocket/Bonnell Springs
  • ✔️ Ventilated Structure
  • ✔️ Responsive Support
  • ✔️ Great for Couples

Memory Foam Mattress

Memory foam molds to your body, relieving pressure and offering superior comfort. Best for people with joint or spine issues.

  • ✔️ Orthopedic Support
  • ✔️ Zero Motion Disturbance
  • ✔️ Body Contouring
  • ✔️ Luxurious Feel

Smart Ortho Hybrid Mattress

Made using special foam with specific construction techniques that cause the mattress to form the shape of the body
